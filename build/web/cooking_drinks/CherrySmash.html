Cherry Smash



DESCRIPTION

Celebrate fresh cherries with this mint-infused cherry cocktail. Black cherry-flavored seltzer
gives the drink the most cherry flavor, but plain seltzer also works well.


INGREDIENTS


1 cup pitted fresh or frozen (thawed) cherries

1/4 cup fresh mint leaves, plus 4 sprigs for garnish

8 teaspoons sugar 		6 ounces (3/4 cup) dark rum

1/4 cup lime juice

3 cups cherry-flavored seltzer

PREPARATION


  1. Divide cherries, mint leaves and sugar among 4 glasses.

  2. Mash everything together with the back of a wooden spoon.

  3. Stir in rum and lime juice. Fill glasses with ice and top with seltzer.

  4. Garnish with mint sprigs.


NUTRITION INFORMATION


Serving size: 1 cocktail

Per serving: 163 calories
0 g fat(0 g sat), 1 g fiber, 16 g carbohydrates, 1 g protein, 9 mcg folate, 0 mg cholesterol,
14 g sugars, 263 IU vitamin A, 8 mg vitamin C, 19 mg calcium,
1 mg iron, 3 mg sodium and 130 mg potassium

Nutrition Bonus: Vitamin C (15% daily value)

Carbohydrate Servings: 1

Exchanges: 1 fruit
