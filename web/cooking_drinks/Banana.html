Banana Smoothie



DESCRIPTION

Based on an Indian yogurt drink called lassi, this healthy fruit smoothie gets its cool flavor from banana with a touch of rose water and nutmeg.


INGREDIENTS


1 1/2 cups ice water

1 ripe banana, peeled and cut into chunks

1/2 cup nonfat plain yogurt 		2 teaspoons honey, or to taste

1/4 teaspoon rose water, (optional)

Pinch nutmeg, preferably freshly grated

PREPARATION


  1. Pinch of nutmeg, preferably freshly grated.

  2. Combine ice water, banana, yogurt, honey and rose water (if using) in a blender.

  3. Blend until smooth. Pour into a large glass and sprinkle nutmeg on top.


NUTRITION INFORMATION


Serving size: 3 cups

Per serving: 217 calories
1 g fat(0 g sat), 3 g fiber, 48 g carbohydrates, 8 g protein, 39 mcg folate, 2 mg cholesterol,
35 g sugars, 12 g added sugars, 84 IU vitamin A, 11 mg vitamin C, 261 mg calcium,
0 mg iron, 107 mg sodium and 746 mg potassium

Nutrition Bonus: Calcium (26% daily value), Vitamin C (19% daily value)

Carbohydrate Servings: 3

Exchanges: 2 fruit, 1 fat-free milk, 1 other carbohydrate
